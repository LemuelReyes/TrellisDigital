About

One of the most crucial mistakes to make as a business owner in this digital age is to have zero web presence.

A 2015 study shows that 68% of Canadians owned a mobile device with the ability to connect to the internet. It is expected to rise to 29.8 million users in Canada alone.

That is a staggering amount of unreached people. Imagine having no front door for your business, what would that be like? Sadly it is true for many, as their digital front doors are nowhere to be found online.

Trellis Digital is a digital agency that builds that front door, as we offer digital solutions for your business. Our services ensure that your business has a web presence, that potential customers can search you online and have an idea of who you are and what services you offer.
